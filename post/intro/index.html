Introduction

Technology in the classroom can help the learner make deeper connections with the material being taught. However, we need to ensure that we choose technologies that enhance the educational experience and do not hinder it. These tools should also fit our pedagogy and allow us to apply proven learning techniques. This site looks at three technologies and assesses then potential use in the classroom.

The technologies chosen were Jupyter Notebooks, Padlet and HackMD. The first is a tool that allows the educator to mix text with code and simulations to help the student learn. The other two technologies are online collaboration tools that can be used by educators and students to collect, organize and distribute information within groups. My interest in these application was to see if I could find a way to enhance the classroom experience of my Engineering Technology students.
